Less Juice Bonus:

What is the Offer?

Every new account is automatically enrolled in our top promotion package, The Less Juice Package. Any switch request after payouts must cover or exceed the total paid-out amount with deposits to be considered for a promotion program switch. Management reserves the right to deny any promotion package switch at its discretion.
